About Us

There's no secret to our sucess. Our company is dedicated to one things- making the world a better place one sweet treat at a time. From our talented bakers to our lovely cashiers to our hard-working delivery team, we put the same love into our work as we want you to get out of your treats. We understand work hard play hard, and we want to bring that mentality to everyone who steps through our doors or hits our order button in the simplest way possible. Because you have enough to worry about in a day.
We are also dedicated for inclusive eating- gluten free, dairy free, and other food sensitivies? Talk to us, we've got you. Taking care of your body shouldn't mean you get to miss out on fulfilling that sweet tooth, and we aim to make sure it never has to!

Let us help you achieve the best part of it- the part when you treat yourself for the hustle. Simply!

Our Founders

Jade and Lucy are two hard-working, hard-playing women who love to laugh as much as they love to hustle. They realized two things: the world needed some help with R&R, and there simply aren't enough sweet treats for alternative diets. So, they turned their culinary hobbies into a career.

Starting from their kitchens only a year and half ago, they started a baked goods delivery service that brought sweets to everyone from tired moms to hungry coorperate parties. Now, as they finally get their brick and mortor place, they aspire to bring the simplicity of self appreciation and care to the local area!
